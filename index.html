Washington University Students for Israel

Welcome to WSI's website. We are Washington University's pro-Israel group on campus and we work tirelessly to ensure that Israel is presented in the best and truthful light. We host a large variety of events, from political discussions to Israeli cooking nights. Take a look around, we hope you like what you see!
