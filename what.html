Our Events

Washington University Students for Israel is proud to be the premier pro-Israel group on our campus. We are one of three Israel-related groups on campus, the others being JStreet and WIPAC. We host all sorts of events, including political and educational speakers, movie nights, photo campaigns, and cooking nights. We hope you will join us at an upcoming event!

Suggest an event idea!

WSI members with Roey Gilad

We hosted an event where Roey Gilad, the Consul General to the Midwest, came and spoke at Wash U about the Midwest's relationship with Israel and the US' relationship with Israel as a whole.

WSI shows their solidarity with Israel

Following a particularly violent bout of fighting between Israel and the surrounding areas in the spring of 2013, WSI called for an emergency session for us to discuss and express our disappointment at the violence of the region.

I stand with Israel because...

WSI set up a table outside of the Danforth University Center in which we had passer-bys let us know why they stand with Israel.

Activities fair

WSI's booth at the fall 2013 student activities fair, where new freshmen learned about our group and signed up to receive our emails and potentially come to our events.

Underpass sign for educational speaker Neil Lazarus

Members of the board of WSI painted the underpass of the South 40 to advertise upcoming speaker Neil Lazarus, who is a world-renowned educator on Israeli affairs and the complexities of the tense situation in the area.

Underpass sign for IDF member Izzy Ezagui

Members of the board of WSI painted the underpass of the South 40 to advertise upcoming speaker Izzy Ezagui, who is an American-born solider in the Israeli army. He lost his arm in battle and yet he returned to the IDF following his injury to fight.
